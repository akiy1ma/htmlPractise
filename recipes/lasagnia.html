Lasagnia recipe

Prep Time: 20 minutes

Cook Time: 1 hour

Servings: 8

Ingredients:

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the noodles: Boil lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. Sauté the meat: Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add diced onion and minced garlic, cooking until softened. Add ground beef, breaking it up and cooking until browned. Drain excess fat, and stir in marinara sauce, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Let it simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Prepare the cheese mixture: In a bowl, combine ricotta, egg, ½ cup Parmesan, salt, and pepper. Mix until smooth.
  5. Layer the lasagna: Spread a thin layer of meat sauce at the bottom of a baking dish. Add a layer of noodles, followed by ricotta mixture, then mozzarella. Repeat the layers, ending with a final layer of noodles topped with remaining sauce and mozzarella.
  6. Bake for 45 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving.

Enjoy your cheesy, savory lasagna!
